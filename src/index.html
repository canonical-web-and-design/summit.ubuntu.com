Welcome to Ubuntu Summit 2022

Join us in Prague, Czech Republic, November 7 to 9 2022, for our first Ubuntu Summit ever. This is the first iteration of our new community focused event, since the last Ubuntu Developer Summit in 2012.

About Ubuntu Summit

Ubuntu Summit is an event focused on Ubuntu and its community.

It is an opportunity for Ubuntu community contributors to speak about their amazing work and success stories to a wide and diverse audience.

We aim to inspire collaboration by showcasing upcoming and ongoing projects created by the community, Canonical, and partners.
